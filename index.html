About Spardha

SPARDHA is the biggest Inter-Collegiate Sports and Entertainment festival of Uttarakhand, organised with the sole aim of promoting sports and social interaction amongst colleges from India. "Valour is stability, not of legs and arms, but of courage and the soul. Winning cannot become your habit unless defeats have torn you apart and you sit in the battle field stitching back yourself one piece at a time laughing in the faces of all defeats. Victory is a matter of the will, a quality of the imagination, Vigour of the emotions. This SPARDHA, get ready for the incursion of Vigour and Valour from the participants for the pursuit of Victory."
